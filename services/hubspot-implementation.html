Frequently Asked Questions

HubSpot implementation questions, answered

How long does a HubSpot implementation take?

Standard implementations take 6–10 weeks from kickoff to launch. Complex migrations with multiple integrations can take 12–16 weeks. We follow the Discovery → Design → Build → Launch → Optimize framework to avoid surprises.

How much does HubSpot implementation cost in 2026?

Direct project cost for a standard Sales + Marketing Hub implementation ranges €15K–€50K depending on complexity, integrations, data migration, and team size. Enterprise tier implementations can exceed €100K. We scope every engagement before quoting.

Do you implement all HubSpot Hubs (Marketing, Sales, Service, Operations, Content)?

Yes. We're HubSpot Platinum Partners with certified consultants across all Hubs. Most implementations cover Sales Hub + Marketing Hub as the core, with Service Hub and Operations Hub added based on your team structure.

What's included in a HubSpot implementation?

Portal configuration, data model and object architecture, pipelines and automation, integrations (Salesforce, Outreach, Apollo, Clay, custom APIs), reporting and dashboards, team training, and a 30-day post-launch optimization window.

Can you migrate us from Salesforce, Pipedrive, or Attio?

Yes. We've migrated 50K+ records from Salesforce to HubSpot with zero downtime. We also handle Pipedrive, Attio, Copper, and spreadsheet-based CRMs. See our CRM migration case study.

What happens after go-live?

We offer a 30-day optimization window in every engagement. Post that, clients typically move to a retainer for ongoing support, or engage us for periodic audits. We don't disappear after launch.
