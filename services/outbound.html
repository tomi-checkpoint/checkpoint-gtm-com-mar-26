Our approach

Four principles we won't negotiate on

Plain-language rules that separate outbound that works from outbound that just adds noise.

01
Inbox health first

Before we send a single email, we set up dedicated domains, warm them properly, and protect your main brand. No shortcuts, no burned domains.

02
Targeting before volume

We'd rather send 50 good emails than 5,000 wrong ones. Tight ICP, real research, and lists your AEs are happy to follow up on.

03
Messaging that sounds human

Short, specific, about the prospect — not you. No “I hope this email finds you well.” No four-paragraph pitches.

04
AEs start conversations, not spam

Your reps should be useful. We build the system so they're spending time on real prospects, not chasing cold inboxes all day.

FAQ

Questions we get before kickoff

Do you do outbound for us, or teach our team?

Both, depending on the engagement. We can run outbound as an embedded team, stand it up and hand it to your reps, or coach your existing SDRs and AEs. We'll recommend what fits your stage.

How long until we see meetings?

Most engagements see first qualified meetings within 4–6 weeks. The first two weeks go into inbox health, targeting, and messaging — rushing that is what burns domains and reps.

Will this burn our domain or brand?

No. We set up dedicated sending infrastructure, warm it properly, and keep volume inside safe limits. Your main domain never takes the risk.

How is this different from hiring an agency?

Most agencies blast volume from their own infrastructure and disappear when the contract ends. We build a system your team owns — targeting logic, messaging, CRM workflows, reporting — so pipeline keeps coming after we leave.

What stack do you work with?

HubSpot, Salesforce, Apollo, Clay, Smartlead, Instantly, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, and the common deliverability tools. We meet your team where they are instead of forcing a rebuild.
