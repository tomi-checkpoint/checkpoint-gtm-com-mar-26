Frequently Asked Questions

Platinum Partner questions, answered

What does Platinum Partner status actually mean?

HubSpot Platinum tier is earned by sustained implementation volume, customer retention, certification depth, and revenue contribution to HubSpot. Practically, it means our consultants hold HubSpot Advanced Admin (and Salesforce Advanced Administrator) credentials, we have direct relationships with HubSpot's sales and solutions engineering teams in DACH and EMEA, and we sit inside pricing and architecture conversations with your HubSpot rep — not on the sidelines after signing.

How does your team coordinate with HubSpot's sales and technical teams?

We join pricing calls with your HubSpot rep from day one. We negotiate Hub tiers, marketing contact buffers (typically 20% over your clean contact count to absorb duplicates and growth), transactional email add-ons for critical system mailings, Service Hub seat counts, and contract extensions on legacy tenants to de-risk cutover timing. On the technical side, we coordinate with HubSpot solutions engineering on data residency, custom object deletion in sandboxes, and platform-level migration steps that only HubSpot can execute.

Can you handle Salesforce to HubSpot migrations?

Yes — this is our most common engagement. We've moved 50K+ records across multi-object migrations covering contacts, companies, deals, products, custom objects, workflows, and historical reporting. We plan the 36-hour automation pause, dedicated IP warming where needed, sub-processor changes, and parallel running so your team keeps selling through the cutover. Our consultants hold Salesforce Advanced Administrator credentials, so we know exactly what we're migrating out of.

Do you implement HubSpot Breeze and AI features?

Yes. We deploy Breeze Intelligence, Breeze Agents, and native HubSpot AI features, and layer Claude, Clay, and custom MCP servers where HubSpot's native AI stops. Typical builds include AI-assisted lead scoring, enrichment orchestration, call summarization, deal notes generation, and conversational intelligence layered on your CRM.

What's your approach to CPQ on HubSpot?

For complex product catalogs — industrial SaaS, manufacturers, regulated bundles — we architect CPQ using HubSpot's products, quotes, and custom objects, integrated with DocuSign or PandaDoc for signature. We model margin structures, discount approval flows, and multi-line quoting. For cases where HubSpot's native CPQ does not fit (rare, but real), we are honest: we tell you, we show you the gap, and we scope a workaround or flag that Salesforce Revenue Cloud may be the better fit. Honesty beats lock-in.

How do you handle GDPR and EU data residency?

We run EU-hosted HubSpot instances for clients that require it, plan server migrations (US to EU) including the 36-hour automation pause, manage sub-processor notifications, and configure data processing agreements, consent records, and cookie governance. We are Berlin-based and default to GDPR-first configurations — not US-first with a European patch.

Can you support enterprise tier deployments?

Yes. Enterprise Sales Hub, Marketing Hub, Service Hub, and Content Hub deployments, including custom objects, business units, hierarchical teams, advanced permissions, field-level permissions, sandbox lifecycles, and enterprise-grade reporting. We handle full multi-hub rollouts with 6 to 16 week timelines depending on scope.

What happens after go-live?

We do not hand you a system and disappear. Every implementation includes a 30-day optimization window. After that, most clients move to an embedded retainer (10 to 30 hours per month) where a Checkpoint consultant sits inside your team as a fractional admin or ops lead — handling change requests, net-new builds, reporting iterations, and quarterly roadmaps.
