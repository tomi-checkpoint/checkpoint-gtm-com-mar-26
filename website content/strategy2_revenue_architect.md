## Section 5: Process

**Overline**: "The Method"

**Headline**:
"Five phases. Each one builds on the last."

**Subheadline**:
We follow a phased methodology that moves from understanding to architecture to implementation to optimization. No phase is skipped. No shortcut is taken.

### Steps

**1. Discovery**
Full systems audit. We map every tool, process, handoff, and data flow across your revenue organization. Stakeholder interviews. Gap analysis. Nothing is assumed.

**2. Design**
Architecture documentation. Data model, object relationships, pipeline stages, automation logic, reporting requirements. The complete blueprint, reviewed and approved before we touch a setting.

**3. Build**
Implementation. CRM configuration, workflow construction, integration architecture, dashboard deployment. Built inside your systems by consultants who understand both the platform and the business logic.

**4. Launch**
Enablement. Team training sessions, process documentation, go-live support, and parallel-run validation. The system is only live when the team is ready.

**5. Optimize**
Iteration. Monthly reporting reviews, workflow refinement, process improvement, and system expansion. The architecture evolves with your business.

### Design Direction
- Numbered step blocks with generous spacing
- Each step has a one-word title + detailed description
- Subtle connecting line or progress indicator between steps
- Scroll-triggered reveal animation

## Section 6: Differentiators

**Block 1: "Architecture before automation"**
We never automate a broken process. Every engagement begins with a systems audit and architecture document. We design the ideal state, validate it with your team, and only then start building. This is why our implementations stick.

**Block 2: "Full-lifecycle, single partner"**
Most agencies cover one layer — outbound, or CRM, or enablement. We architect the complete revenue system: acquisition, conversion, retention, and expansion. One partner who sees the whole picture and owns the integration between layers.

**Block 3: "Built for European SaaS"**
Berlin-based. GDPR-aware by default. Multilingual delivery in English, German, and Spanish. We understand European buying cycles, compliance requirements, and the specific challenges of scaling a DACH-native company internationally.
