## Section 5: Process

**Overline**: "How It Works"

**Headline**:
"From zero to operating model. Here's the path."

**Subheadline**:
Most of our engagements start with a workshop and end with a fully operational revenue system. Here's what that looks like week by week.

### Steps

**1. Discovery (Week 1–2)**
We audit everything: your CRM, your processes, your team structure, your tools. We interview the people doing the work. We map the gaps.

**2. Design (Week 2–3)**
Customer journey document. Pipeline architecture. Data model. Automation logic. Everything designed on paper and validated with your team before we configure a single setting.

**3. Build (Week 3–8)**
Hands-on implementation. We're in your HubSpot, building the system, configuring automations, creating dashboards. You see progress in real time.

**4. Launch (Week 8–10)**
Training sessions. Process documentation. Go-live support. Your team is ready to run the system, not just aware it exists.

**5. Optimize (Ongoing)**
Monthly reviews, reporting refinements, new automation as you scale. We stay as long as you need us and leave a system that runs without us.

### Design Direction
- Timeline format with week indicators adds specificity
- Numbered steps with generous vertical spacing
- Communicates speed — "zero to operating model" framing
- Subtle progress bar or timeline connector

## Section 10: Engagement Models

**Overline**: "Start Where You Are"

**Headline**:
"Pick the model that matches your stage"

**Subheadline**:
Early-stage? Start with a workshop. Growing fast? Go fractional. Somewhere in between? We'll figure it out on the discovery call.

### Cards (4)

**Project-Based**
A defined sprint with a clear outcome. HubSpot implementation, outbound engine build, or CRM migration. Typical timeline: 4–12 weeks.

**Retainer**
Monthly hours for ongoing support. Pipeline reviews, system optimization, new automations, ad-hoc builds. Flexible scope that grows with you.

**Fractional / Embedded**
A RevOps lead embedded in your team. Joins your standups, owns your systems, runs your pipeline reviews. All the value of a senior hire, none of the 6-month recruiting cycle.

**Workshop / Training**
A focused session to level up your team. HubSpot adoption, sales methodology, outbound training, or RevOps fundamentals. Often the starting point for a deeper engagement.

### Design Direction
- 4-column card grid
- Casual but clear language
- "Start where you are" framing removes commitment anxiety
- Cards hint at natural progression (workshop → project → retainer → embedded)
