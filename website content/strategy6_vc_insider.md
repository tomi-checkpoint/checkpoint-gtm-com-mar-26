## Section 5: Process

**Overline**: "The Engagement Timeline"

**Headline**:
"Board-ready revenue infrastructure in 90 days"

**Subheadline**:
We move fast because the pattern is known. Every engagement follows a five-phase methodology refined across 50+ B2B SaaS companies at similar stages.

### Steps

**1. Discovery (Week 1–2)**
Systems audit, process mapping, stakeholder interviews. We diagnose the current state against what we've seen work at your stage across dozens of similar companies.

**2. Design (Week 2–3)**
Target-state architecture. Customer journey, data model, pipeline stages, automation logic. Documented, reviewed, and benchmarked against companies at your stage and vertical.

**3. Build (Week 3–8)**
Implementation. CRM configuration, workflow construction, integration architecture, reporting dashboards. Built in your systems, validated against your data, refined with your team.

**4. Launch (Week 8–10)**
Team enablement. Role-specific training, process documentation, go-live support. Your team is operational on the new system — not just informed about it.

**5. Optimize (Ongoing)**
Iteration cycles. Monthly performance reviews, workflow refinement, new capability rollouts. The system evolves as you scale from one stage to the next.

### Design Direction
- Week markers on each phase add specificity
- "Board-ready in 90 days" headline speaks investor language
- "Benchmarked against companies at your stage" reinforces pattern-matching
- Numbered timeline format

## Section 10: Engagement Models

**Overline**: "Flexible Models for Every Stage"

**Headline**:
"From first CRM to full operating model — engagement models that grow with you"

**Subheadline**:
Most portfolio companies start with a project or workshop, then expand to retainer or embedded as they scale. We designed the models to make that transition seamless.

### Cards (4)

**Project-Based**
A defined engagement with clear deliverables. HubSpot implementation, CRM migration, outbound engine build. Typical for post-raise infrastructure sprints. 4–12 weeks.

**Retainer**
Monthly operational partnership. Pipeline management, system optimization, reporting, and ad-hoc builds. Ideal for companies between rounds who need continuous RevOps without a full-time hire.

**Fractional / Embedded**
A senior RevOps operator on your team. Attends standups, owns pipeline operations, manages the CRM. The "VP RevOps" your board wants you to have, without the 6-month search.

**Workshop / Training**
Discovery workshops, sales methodology training, HubSpot enablement. Often the starting point for VC portfolio companies exploring what they need.

### Design Direction
- 4-column card grid
- "Post-raise infrastructure sprints" and "between rounds" language is VC-native
- Cards suggest a natural progression path
- Clean, stage-aware design
