## Section 5: Process

**Overline**: "The Migration & Implementation Method"

**Headline**:
"A proven methodology. Zero data loss. Zero downtime."

**Subheadline**:
Every implementation and migration follows our five-phase methodology. Each phase has documented deliverables, stakeholder checkpoints, and rollback protocols.

### Steps

**1. Discovery & Audit**
Full audit of your current CRM, data model, integrations, and processes. We map every object, property, automation, and report. We interview every team that touches the system.

**2. Architecture Design**
Target-state documentation. New data model, object relationships, pipeline definitions, automation logic, integration architecture, and migration mapping. Reviewed and signed off before we start.

**3. Build & Migrate**
Implementation in your target HubSpot portal. Data transformation, field mapping, historical data import, workflow reconstruction, dashboard configuration. Parallel-run validation against your existing system.

**4. Launch & Enablement**
Cutover execution. Team training by role. Process documentation. Go-live support with dedicated escalation path. Your team is proficient on day one.

**5. Optimize & Expand**
Post-launch iteration. Data quality monitoring, workflow refinement, reporting expansion, and new capability rollouts. The system grows as your team grows.

### Design Direction
- Numbered steps with technical depth
- Emphasis on methodology and safety (zero data loss, rollback protocols)
- Reassuring tone for what is typically a high-anxiety project
- Subtle connecting lines between phases
