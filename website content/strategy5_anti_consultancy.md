## Section 5: Process

**Overline**: "How It Actually Works"

**Headline**:
"No mystery. No scope creep. Five phases, every engagement."

**Subheadline**:
We're transparent about the process because we've refined it across 40+ engagements. Every phase has clear deliverables, stakeholder checkpoints, and a definition of done.

### Steps

**1. Discovery**
We audit everything — systems, processes, team workflows, data quality. Not to produce a findings report. To build the list of what we're going to fix, in priority order.

**2. Design**
We document the target state — data model, pipeline stages, automation logic, integration architecture. You review it. You approve it. Then we build exactly that.

**3. Build**
We implement it. In your HubSpot. With your data. Not in a sandbox. Not in a slide deck. In the live system your team uses every day.

**4. Launch**
We train your team by role, document every process, and support the go-live with dedicated escalation. Nobody is left reading a manual.

**5. Optimize**
We stay for the iteration. Monthly reviews, workflow refinement, reporting improvements, new capabilities. Because launch is the starting line, not the finish line.

### Design Direction
- Each step contrasts what Checkpoint does vs. what others do
- "Not in a sandbox. Not in a slide deck." — copy reinforces the anti-consultancy thesis at every turn
- Numbered steps, vertical timeline
- Scroll-driven reveal

## Section 6: Differentiators

**Block 1: "Operators, not advisors"**
Our team joins your Slack, attends your standups, and logs into your HubSpot. We don't produce recommendations from arm's length. We do the work, inside your systems, alongside your team. When we leave, the systems stay. The processes stay. The expertise stays.

**Block 2: "Same team, start to finish"**
No bait-and-switch. The people on the discovery call are the people building your system. No junior associates, no offshore implementation team, no "delivery partner." The strategist is the builder.

**Block 3: "We price in the implementation"**
Our proposals include every hour of strategy, implementation, training, and optimization. There's no moment where the "real cost" appears as a change order. The number you see is the number you pay.

### Design Direction
- Dark/alt background section
- 3-column grid
- Each block addresses a specific frustration buyers have with consulting firms
- Direct, punchy copy — no wasted words

Monthly operational partnership. Ongoing system management, pipeline optimization, new automation builds, and strategic guidance. One invoice. No surprise line items.
