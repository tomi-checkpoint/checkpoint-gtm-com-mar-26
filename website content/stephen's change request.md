## Change Summary

Adds automatic delta calculation for upsell/downsell opportunities. When a ramp is linked to a UPS/DWN opp, the service compares CB Products (before state) against ramp line items (after state) and creates Upsell_Delta_Line_Item__c records showing per-product before/after/delta breakdown. The total delta is written to a new Upsell_Delta_Amount__c field on Opportunity.

Components:
- New custom object: Upsell_Delta_Line_Item__c (20 fields including ramp metadata, before/after/delta, effective date)
- New field: Opportunity.Upsell_Delta_Amount__c
- New Apex class: UpsellDeltaCalculationService (extends TriggerSubscriptionsService.TriggerHandler)
- New trigger: CbRampTrigger (after insert/update on CB_Ramp__c)
- Extended: CbRampLineItemTrigger (added after update/delete)
- New test class: UpsellDeltaCalculationServiceTest
- New PermissionSet: Upsell_Delta_Access
