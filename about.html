About Checkpoint GTM

We're the ops team you wish you had

Checkpoint GTM is a Berlin-based RevOps and GTM consultancy for B2B SaaS companies. We embed into client teams to build the systems, processes, and infrastructure that make revenue repeatable. Strategy tied to implementation. Every time.

Most consultancies deliver strategy decks and walk away. We do the opposite. We embed into your team, build the systems ourselves, and stay until it's working. Every recommendation comes with implementation. Every strategy becomes a system.
