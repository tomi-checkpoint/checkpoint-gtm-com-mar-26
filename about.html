About Checkpoint GTM

We're the ops team you wish you had

Checkpoint GTM is a Berlin-based RevOps and GTM consultancy for B2B SaaS companies. We embed into client teams to build the systems, processes, and infrastructure that make revenue repeatable. Strategy tied to implementation. Every time.

What Makes Us Different

Not another agency

Most consultancies deliver strategy decks and walk away. We do the opposite. We embed into your team, build the systems ourselves, and stay until it's working. Every recommendation comes with implementation. Every strategy becomes a system.

Embedded Model

We work inside your team, not outside it. Slack channels, standups, and shared dashboards — not monthly reports.

Implementation-First

We don't just tell you what to do. We configure HubSpot, build the automations, clean the data, and train your team.

Stage-Appropriate

A seed-stage company needs different GTM infrastructure than a Series B. We right-size every recommendation.

Multilingual & EMEA-Aware

Based in Berlin, working across Europe and globally. We understand DACH, EU compliance, and cross-border GTM.
