FAQ

HubSpot & RevOps in Berlin — answered

Is Checkpoint GTM a HubSpot Platinum Partner?

Yes. Checkpoint GTM is a certified HubSpot Platinum Partner with Advanced Admin credentials across Sales, Marketing, Service, and Operations Hubs. Platinum status means we meet HubSpot's bar on certifications, customer outcomes, and retained MRR — not just a logo on a pitch deck. Our team also carries Salesforce Advanced Admin certification, which is relevant when we're migrating Berlin scaleups off Salesforce or running both platforms in parallel during a transition.

What does a HubSpot Partner in Berlin do?

A HubSpot Partner in Berlin typically handles implementation, CRM migration, RevOps design, marketing ops, customer success ops, sales enablement, and automation work on top of HubSpot. Checkpoint GTM does all of that and stays embedded afterwards — which is the part that matters. We configure your portal, architect your data model, migrate your pipelines, build your workflows, integrate your stack, train your team, and then keep operating the system alongside you instead of handing over a PDF and leaving.

How much does HubSpot implementation cost in Germany?

HubSpot implementation costs in Germany vary significantly with scope and company stage. A seed-stage setup for a single Hub with clean data and a lifecycle definition typically lands in the low five figures. A Series A or B multi-Hub rollout with a CRM migration from Salesforce or Pipedrive, full attribution, and cross-EU compliance work is meaningfully larger. We publish transparent packages and engagement models — project-based, retainer, fractional/embedded, and workshop — so you can see exactly what you're paying for before any contract. The best way to get a real number is a 30-minute scoping call.

Do you work with non-Berlin clients?

Yes — about two thirds of our work happens outside Berlin. We operate across 12+ countries and regularly support teams in London, Amsterdam, Paris, Milan, Munich, Zurich, and the Nordics, plus US clients with European operations. Berlin is our HQ and a natural center of gravity, but the embedded model works the same way remotely: we sit in your Slack, ship on your timezone, and show up for quarterly reviews in person when it matters.

What languages does your team work in?

We deliver in five languages: American English, British English, German, Italian, and French. That's not a marketing claim — it's how we actually staff engagements. German-speaking buyers get German discovery calls. French scaleups get French training sessions. Italian teams get Italian documentation when they need it. For pan-European RevOps rollouts, this lets us run a single program across DACH, the UK, France, Italy, and the Nordics without losing nuance in translation.

How quickly can you start a HubSpot implementation?

For most Berlin B2B SaaS teams, we can kick off within 1–2 weeks of signing. A typical Discovery phase takes 2–3 weeks, Design and Build runs 4–8 weeks depending on scope, and Launch happens once your team is trained and the data passes QA. For urgent situations — a CRM migration blocking a board meeting, a broken attribution model before Q-end, a portfolio-wide rollout — we can often compress the first phases significantly. Tell us the deadline on the intro call and we'll be straight about what's realistic.
