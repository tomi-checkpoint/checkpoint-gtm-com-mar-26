Agency overview

Checkpoint GTM is a Berlin-based RevOps and GTM consultancy for B2B SaaS companies, startups, and VC-backed teams. We embed into client teams to build the operating layer behind growth — strategy tied to implementation, every time. We are HubSpot Platinum Solutions Partners and Salesforce Advanced Administrator certified.

  • Audiences: seed and Series A/B founders, revenue and marketing leaders, customer success and ops leaders, VC platform teams, companies migrating to HubSpot, buyers exploring AI and automation in GTM.
  • Coverage: EMEA-native (Berlin HQ), serves North America. 12+ countries, 4 delivery languages: EN, DE, IT, FR.
  • Differentiators: embedded operator model (not strategy theater); implementation depth; technical plus business acumen; stage-appropriate solutions; transparent packages.

How we engage

  • Project-based — fixed scope, defined timeline. Typical range €15K–€90K.
  • Retainer — ongoing monthly support and operating cadence. Typical range €6K–€25K/month.
  • Fractional / Embedded — senior operators inside your team for a defined period.
  • Workshop / Training — focused team enablement: HubSpot, RevOps, AI for GTM.

Process

Every engagement runs through the same five-phase delivery model:

  • Discovery — interviews, system audit, data model review, current-state map.
  • Design — operating model, pipeline structure, lifecycle, attribution, automation blueprint.
  • Build — implementation in HubSpot (or target system), integrations, data migration, automation, dashboards.
  • Launch — enablement, training, go-live cutover, hyper-care.
  • Optimize — measurement, iteration, ongoing operating cadence with the team.
