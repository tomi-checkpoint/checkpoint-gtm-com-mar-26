Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about RevOps & GTM consulting

What's the difference between RevOps consulting and a marketing agency?

RevOps consulting builds the operational infrastructure (CRM, data, processes, tooling) that marketing, sales, and CS all run on. An agency executes campaigns. We handle the system; you or your agency handle the campaigns.

How much does a Checkpoint GTM engagement cost?

Project-based engagements start at €15K for focused scope (e.g., a HubSpot portal audit or CRM migration). Retainers typically range €6K–€20K/month depending on scope. Fractional RevOps engagements are priced by commitment hours. We share full pricing after a 30-minute discovery call.

How fast can you start?

Most engagements kick off within two weeks of signing. Urgent projects (e.g., time-sensitive CRM migrations) can start within 48 hours if scoping is clear.

Do you work with pre-seed or early-stage startups?

Yes. We have workshop packages and lean implementation engagements designed for seed-stage GTM chaos. Our sweet spot is Series A–B, but we support pre-seed through Series C.

What languages does your team work in?

English, German, Italian, and French. We're Berlin-based with native EMEA coverage.

Is Checkpoint GTM a HubSpot Partner?

Yes — HubSpot Platinum Partner with Advanced Admin certified consultants. We're also Salesforce Advanced Administrator certified for migrations.
